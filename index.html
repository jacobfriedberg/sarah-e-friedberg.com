Sarah Friedberg

Sarah Friedberg is a student at the Portledge School in Locust Valley, NY. Her previous books include Maybe at Dawn, When Light Touches Sky, Waiting for Dunno, and The Girl Who Wished Through A Mirror. She studied creative writing at Dartmouth and acting at Stagedoor Manor in Loch Sheldrake, NY. When We Were Briefly Strangers is her first collection of plays.
